BD Fact Check

BD FactCheck is a nonprofit company registered in Illinois, USA under Section 501 (c) (3). The scope of our work is Bangladeshi Diaspora living in Bangladesh and different countries of the world. Our fact check team is based in Bangladesh and the United States and works independently. We are adamant on the question of truth and objectivity.

BD FactCheck is the first fact finding company in Bangladesh. It is a non-profit, non-political and professional organization that aims to reduce the level of lies, suspicions, deceptions and mistakes in various internal affairs of Bangladesh. BD Factcheck mainly conducts fact checks on four issues: public figures, various organizations including political parties, mass media, and social media. We only check the facts on the issues that the speeches reach the people through any discussion meeting, press conference, public meeting or the media. BD FactCheck also checks fake news, pictures or videos that go viral on social media.
